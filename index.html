FAQ

How do I care for my jewelry?

To keep your jewelry looking its best, follow these care instructions:

  • Regular Cleaning: Clean your jewelry regularly using a soft, lint-free cloth to remove dirt, oils, and fingerprints.
  • Avoid Harsh Chemicals: Avoid exposing your jewelry to harsh chemicals such as bleach, chlorine, and cleaning agents, as they can damage the metal and gemstones.
  • Storage: Store your jewelry in a clean, dry place away from sunlight, moisture, and extreme temperatures. Use jewelry boxes or pouches to prevent scratching and tangling.
  • Separate Storage: Store each piece of jewelry separately to prevent scratching and tangling. Use jewelry dividers or pouches for earrings, necklaces, bracelets, and rings.
  • Avoid Contact: Remove your jewelry before engaging in activities such as swimming, showering, exercising, or sleeping to prevent damage and tarnishing.
  • Professional Inspection: Periodically, have your jewelry inspected by a professional jeweler to check for loose stones, worn prongs, or other issues that may require repair.

Following these care instructions will help preserve the beauty and longevity of your jewelry for years to come.

How do I determine my ring size?

Follow these steps to determine your ring size:

  1. Use a Ring Sizer: Visit a jewelry store or request a ring sizer online to accurately measure your ring size.
  2. Measure an Existing Ring: If you have a ring that fits well, measure its inner diameter with a ruler and use a ring size chart to find your size.
  3. Use a Printable Ring Sizer: Print a ring size chart from a reputable jewelry website and follow the instructions to measure your finger.
  4. Visit a Jeweler: Visit a local jeweler for professional assistance in determining your ring size.

Ensuring the correct ring size will help you find the perfect fit for your jewelry.
